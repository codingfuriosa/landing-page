Lake Life Township

OAS Lake Life Township is a residential development situated in the tranquil locality of Joka, South Kolkata. Spanning across 75 acres, the township offers a mix of 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments and villas, with sizes ranging from 429 to 1,365 sq. ft. The project is designed to provide a harmonious living experience, blending modern amenities with natural surroundings. As of September 2023, the township has been completed and is ready for possession.
